Our commitment

Every consignment, every kilometre, every shift — planned, monitored and delivered safely.

Heavy vehicle transport is one of the most safety-critical industries in Australia, and Western Australia's distances raise the stakes further. LOWKI's approach is systematic: qualification before the keys, technology inside every cab, and scheduling that treats fatigue law as a floor rather than a target.

Because we operate inside Tier 1 networks, our safety systems are regularly exposed to the audit and induction standards of Australia's largest logistics companies — a discipline that flows through to every client we serve, from government departments to a regional shopfront.

Driver qualification framework

Qualified before the first kilometre.

MODULE 01

Driver awareness

Defensive driving, hazard perception and WA road-condition awareness — refreshed regularly, not once at induction.

MODULE 02

Chain of Responsibility

Every driver understands CoR duties — theirs, ours and the client's — and how they apply to loading, speed and hours.

MODULE 03

Load restraint

Qualified to current load restraint standards across every vehicle class, from van cargo nets to B-double gates and straps.

MODULE 04

Fatigue management

Trained in fatigue law, personal fatigue indicators and rest planning — supported by monitored scheduling.

MODULE 05

Onboard technology

Competent on in-cab systems: GPS, fatigue monitoring, safety-sense alerts and electronic work diaries.

MODULE 06

Client inductions

Site- and client-specific inductions completed before on-demand drivers enter your operation.

Fatigue management in practice

Long distances, managed properly.

WA linehaul routinely exceeds 1,000 km. Our fatigue system combines planning and technology so those kilometres are covered safely:

  • Runs planned within regulated hours with rest built into the schedule, not squeezed around it
  • In-cab fatigue monitoring systems providing real-time alerts
  • Two-driver changeovers on extreme-distance and urgent night work
  • The 24/7 desk monitoring progress and re-planning when conditions change
