~100 TEAM MEMBERS · GROWING STATEWIDE
Working here

Around 100 people. Nobody is a number.

Transport companies love to say “our people are our greatest asset” — usually in the same year they cut penalty rates. LOWKI's version is more concrete: above-award pay across every classification, training that happens on the clock and on us, and rosters built by a fatigue-management system rather than by wishful thinking. The operations managers you'd work under came up through the same seats, which changes how problems get solved at 2am.

The next decade makes it more interesting. Half the fleet is transitioning to full electric within twelve months, which means LOWKI drivers and technicians will be among the first in WA working electric heavy vehicles at fleet scale — skills the whole industry will be hiring for. Whether you're a van driver looking at an HR pathway, an MC linehauler who wants kilometres with proper rest planning, or a technician curious about high-voltage systems, the growth here is real and mapped.
