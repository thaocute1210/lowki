The challenge

A growing Esperance-region wholesaler needed dependable weekly freight from Perth — with tail-lift delivery, because their site has no dock or forklift.

The LOWKI solution

LOWKI designed a scheduled weekly service through the Esperance depot: metro pickup, regional linehaul and safe ground-level tail-lift delivery, with one point of contact who knows the account. The service scales with their seasonal volumes.
