ABOUT THE VIDEOS — IMPORTANT
  The three video files supplied with the brief are Terminal Industries'
  own pre-rendered 3D marketing assets. They cannot legally be published
  on the LOWKI website, so they were NOT included.
  Instead, assets/video/loop-1.mp4 … loop-3.mp4 are ORIGINAL animated
  loops generated in LOWKI's brand palette (each under 250 KB, fast to
  load). They are safe to publish as-is.
  RECOMMENDED: when you have professional footage of your own trucks,
  depots and drivers (or licensed stock), simply overwrite loop-1.mp4,
  loop-2.mp4 and loop-3.mp4 — every page updates automatically. Ideal
  specs: 1920x1080, H.264, 6–10 s seamless loop, no audio, < 5 MB each.

HOW TO VIEW / DEPLOY
  Open index.html in a browser. Deploy by uploading the folder to any
  host (cPanel, Netlify, Vercel, GitHub Pages). No build step.

BEFORE GOING LIVE
  1. contact.html — replace "[Add phone number]" / "[Add email address]".
  2. about.html — replace the six "[Team member name]" cards with real
     names, bios and photos.
  3. privacy.html / terms.html — legal review, add ABN and dates.
  4. quote.html / contact.html forms — connect to Formspree, Netlify
     Forms or your email backend (currently demo mode via data-demo).
  5. Replace video loops with your own footage (see above).
  6. news.html — link starter articles to real posts as you publish.

ACCESSIBILITY & PERFORMANCE NOTES
  * All motion (smooth scroll, reveals, parallax, video pinning) is
    disabled automatically for users with "reduced motion" enabled.
  * Videos are muted, loop, lazy-play only while on screen, and pause
    off-screen.
  * Fully responsive to mobile; keyboard focus styles included.
