Sector overview

Every carrier hits capacity. The good ones have a partner ready.

A won contract that outgrows the fleet, a prime mover in the workshop, three drivers on leave in the same fortnight — every transport operator knows these weeks. LOWKI provides subcontract and overflow capacity to fellow WA carriers with the professionalism the arrangement demands: your customer relationship stays yours, our equipment and drivers meet your standards, and the engagement is handled with complete confidentiality.
