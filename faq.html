FAQ · STRAIGHT ANSWERS

Questions we get asked on the phone, answered here.

Booking, coverage, fleet, on-demand supply and safety — if it's not here, our 24/7 desk will know.

Booking & quotes
Use the online quote form, call our 24/7 operations desk, or email us. For standard lanes we usually respond the same day; urgent hotshots are quoted by phone in minutes.
Origin and destination, freight description, weight and dimensions (or pallet count), any access constraints (no dock, tail-lift needed), and your delivery window.
Both. From a single urgent pallet to multi-year dedicated runs — flexible freight solutions for small and large consignments is core to what we do.
Coverage & timing
All of Western Australia. Metro Perth from our Helena Valley HQ, regional WA via depots in Narrogin, Esperance and Tom Price, and remote areas through our partner network.
Yes — LOWKI runs 24/7. Night linehaul, after-hours delivery windows and urgent dispatch at any hour.
A dedicated hotshot vehicle can usually be rolling within hours of confirmation, running direct to destination — including 1,000 km+ regional runs.
Fleet & capability
Approximately 60 vehicles: flat-top and curtain-sided semi-trailers, B-double combinations, heavy rigid trucks with tail-lift capability, and vans/light vehicles.
Yes — for sites without docks or forklifts, metro and regional. Tell us the heaviest single item so we can assign the right capacity.
Every vehicle carries live GPS tracking, and our operations desk provides proactive updates on time-critical runs.
On-demand fleet & drivers
We provide qualified drivers — trained in CoR, load restraint, fatigue management and onboard technology — with or without vehicles, at short notice, to cover leave, surges or equipment downtime.
Yes. Our drivers are inducted into client processes so they run your routes, your paperwork and your standards.
No long lock-ins — cover one shift or a full season.
Hotshots & urgent freight
A dedicated vehicle for your consignment only — direct from pickup to destination with no consolidation and no depot stops. It's the fastest legal way to move urgent freight long distances in WA.
Any hour. The desk is staffed 24/7 and night dispatch is routine — a 9pm call can have a vehicle rolling before midnight.
You're buying the whole vehicle for the run, so pricing reflects distance, vehicle class and crewing (two-driver for extreme distances). We quote it by phone in minutes.
Accounts, pricing & invoicing
By lane, weight/volume (or pallet count), vehicle class and access requirements. Dedicated runs and on-demand supply are priced per engagement. Every quote covers the full movement — no hidden legs.
Yes — regular shippers can apply for a credit account via our Resources page. Approved accounts receive agreed payment terms and consolidated invoicing.
Any applicable levies are disclosed in your quote up front. The price we quote is the price you're invoiced, unless the job changes.
Safety & compliance
All drivers are qualified in driver awareness, Chain of Responsibility, load restraint and fatigue management. The fleet carries fatigue monitoring systems, safety-sense technology and GPS tracking.
Yes — CoR compliance is embedded in scheduling, loading and documentation on every movement.
LOWKI is transitioning 50% of the current fleet from diesel to full electric over the next 12 months, as part of our go-green commitment.
