Executive overview

LOWKI is the operating partner behind some of the most demanding freight networks in Western Australia — reliable at scale, flexible on demand.

Established in August 2015 to fulfil dedicated freight runs for Toll Group, LOWKI's consistent performance has earned engagements across multiple Tier 1 logistics providers — CTI Logistics, Centurion, Linfox and Team Global Express — alongside ad-hoc and project freight for the mining sector and government.

Today the company operates approximately 60 vehicles supported by a workforce of around 100 personnel, deployed from a primary metropolitan depot in Helena Valley and regional facilities in Narrogin, Esperance and Tom Price. This footprint, extended by established operational partnerships throughout regional WA, enables coordinated delivery to virtually any location in the state — including remote and hard-to-reach areas.

LOWKI's differentiator is continuity: the capacity to supply qualified drivers and fully equipped vehicles at short notice, absorbing driver shortages, seasonal surges and equipment downtime so client operations proceed without disruption.
