In depth

Most delivery points in WA don't have a dock.

It surprises people who work in distribution centres: the majority of real-world delivery points — shopfronts, clinics, farm sheds, small warehouses, site offices — have no dock, no forklift, and often no one able to lift what's arriving. Freight networks built for dock-to-dock transfer handle these destinations badly, which is why tail-lift capability is a first-class service at LOWKI rather than an afterthought surcharge.

Our tail-lift equipped heavy rigids and vans deliver pallets and heavy single items safely to ground level, with an onboard pallet jack for positioning where access and safety allow. Operators are trained in load restraint and manual handling, and the capability extends across the entire statewide network — a Narrogin pharmacy and a Perth shopfront get the same safe, dock-free delivery. Booking is simple: tell us the heaviest single item and any access constraints, and we assign the right capacity first time.
